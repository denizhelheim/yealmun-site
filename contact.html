YEALMUN Rules of Procedure

Standard MUN rules adapted for our conference

General Rules

All delegates must adhere to these procedures

1. Roll Call

At the start of each session, the Chair calls the roll. Delegates respond with "Present" or "Present and Voting".

2. Speaker's List

Delegates may add their name to the speaker's list to address the committee. Speeches are limited to 90 seconds.

3. Points and Motions

Delegates may raise points (Point of Order, Point of Inquiry) or propose motions (Moderated Caucus, Unmoderated Caucus, Suspension of the Meeting).

4. Voting Procedure

All substantive matters are decided by a simple majority. For draft resolutions, a two‑thirds majority may be required for certain amendments.

5. Decorum

All delegates must maintain diplomatic behaviour. Offensive language or disruptive actions will result in a warning or removal from the committee.

Full ROP document will be provided to all registered delegates.
