About YEALMUN

Our philosophy, vision, and global impact

What is YEALMUN?

YEALMUN (Young Explorers And Leaders Model United Nations) is a prestigious conference where students simulate UN procedures, hone their public speaking, and develop leadership skills. We bring together high school and university students every year.

Our mission is to raise awareness about global challenges, encourage critical thinking, and foster peaceful solutions. We believe in the power of youth to shape a better world.

Our Mission

To provide an authentic diplomatic environment that strengthens multilateral cooperation and empowers young leaders.

Our Vision

To become one of Turkey’s leading MUN conferences, known for sustainability, inclusivity, and innovation.
